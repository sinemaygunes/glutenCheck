Who Should Follow a Gluten-Free Diet?

Individuals with celiac disease must completely avoid gluten. This disease causes serious damage to the intestines when gluten is consumed.

People with gluten intolerance or sensitivity may experience symptoms such as indigestion, gas, and abdominal pain when they consume gluten.

Additionally, some individuals with autoimmune diseases and those who suspect gluten sensitivity may choose to follow a gluten-free diet.

Before starting a gluten-free diet, it is essential to consult with a healthcare professional unless it is medically necessary.
