Gluten Intolerance Symptoms

The following symptoms are commonly observed in people with gluten intolerance or sensitivity:

  • Bloating, gas, abdominal pain
  • Digestive issues such as diarrhea or constipation
  • Headaches and migraines
  • Chronic fatigue
  • Joint and muscle pain
  • Skin rashes (e.g., eczema)
  • Depression and anxiety
  • Iron deficiency and anemia

If these symptoms appear after consuming gluten-containing foods, it is recommended to consult a healthcare professional.
